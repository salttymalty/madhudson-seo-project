The short version.

Five same-day wins cost nothing and change nothing visually. Trust signals and product content expansion are the biggest revenue levers. Schema markup and AEO positioning give Mad Hudson first-mover advantage.

What the research found

12 months of Shopify data, every product page audited, site structure compared against 11 other watch brands. Strong product, strong brand, genuine audience. The gap is in how the site talks to search engines.

Most of the fixes are invisible to customers. They happen in meta descriptions, alt text, page titles, structured data — the parts of the site that humans don't read but algorithms do.

Five same-day fixes

  • Add alt text to every product image Products → click image → Alt text
  • Write custom meta descriptions Products → Search engine listing → Description
  • Update page titles with artist names Same section → Page title
  • Enable bot filtering Analytics → Settings → Exclude known bots
  • Add UTM parameters to link-in-bio ?utm_source=instagram&utm_medium=social&utm_campaign=linkinbio
