Time as container, not countdown

Most timers count down. Numbers falling, creating urgency. The Focus Bezel works differently.

Twist the bezel to align the colored sector with where your minute hand is now. That sector becomes a container — a defined space for your attention. You don't watch numbers decrease. You watch your hand travel through a territory you've claimed.

When it exits the color, the session is complete. No buzzing. No interruption. Just the quiet satisfaction of time well held.

How it works

1

Twist

Rotate the bezel to align the colored sector with your current minute hand position.

2

Work

The sector holds your session. No need to check constantly — time is contained.

3

Complete

When the minute hand exits the colored zone, your session is done. Simple as that.

For different relationships with time

Some of us don't feel time the same way. The hours slip, or they stretch. Deadlines arrive without warning. Sessions blur into marathons.

The Focus Bezel doesn't fix this. It doesn't optimize you. It simply makes time visible and contained — something you can see and touch, rather than something that happens to you.

A lens for relating to time, not a tool for self-improvement.
