## üíå How to Send Pitches

### **Email Subject Line**
Pick ONE from press-report-february.md (customize with watch name + artist name):
- "Artist-as-Author Watch Collaboration [Watch Name] √ó [Artist Name]"
- Or similar angle from templates

### **Email Body**
1. **Personalization (1-2 sentences)** - Reference something they covered recently
   - "I noticed your recent coverage of emerging artists building community..."
2. **The Hook (2-3 sentences)** - What's the story?
   - "Mad Hudson positions artists as authors, not endorsers..."
3. **The Angle (2-3 sentences)** - Why this publication, this angle?
   - "Your readers care about authentic design. Here's a story about..."
4. **Offer (1-2 sentences)** - What you're providing
   - "I have interview access + high-res imagery if you'd like to explore this."
5. **CTA (1 sentence)** - Ask for response
   - "Would you be interested in a feature story?"

**See `press-tracking-template.md` for full email template (copy-paste ready)**

### **Attachments**
- 3-4 strongest high-res images
- NOTHING ELSE (keep email clean)

### **Send Timing**
- **Morning preferred** (editors check email first thing)
- **Tuesday-Thursday best** (not Monday inbox overload, not Friday distraction)
- **One pitch per day maximum** (don't spam same editor twice)

## üìä How to Track Responses

**Use the Google Sheet you created.**

**Status codes:**
- **Sent** = Waiting for response
- **Interested** = They asked for materials / want to discuss
- **In Progress** = Assigned to writer, feature in development
- **Published** = Article live (capture URL + backlink)
- **Declined** = They passed (don't follow up)
- **No Response** = 4+ weeks, no reply (close it out)

**Follow-up rules:**
1. **2 weeks after pitch:** If no response, send ONE brief follow-up email
2. **4 weeks after pitch:** If still no response, mark as "No Response," close out
3. **Never send more than 2 emails to same editor** (1 pitch + 1 follow-up max)

See `press-tracking-template.md` for response playbooks (what to do if interested, declined, etc.)

## üö® Common Mistakes to Avoid

‚ùå **Generic pitches** ‚Üí Editors get hundreds. Personalize or delete.

‚ùå **Spamming with follow-ups** ‚Üí 1 pitch + 1 follow-up only. If they want you, they'll respond.

‚ùå **Sending too much stuff** ‚Üí Keep initial pitch to 200-250 words + 3-4 images. Don't attach press kit (they'll ask).

‚ùå **Wrong angle** ‚Üí See `press-research-recent-coverage.md` for what each publication values. Match the angle.

‚ùå **Wrong editor** ‚Üí Don't send design story to tech writer. Research who covers what.
